The riddle

'What makes you who you are?'

Brief

In this project, I had to produce a short digital video that can be distributed through different online channels. This project had as a goal the attaining of basic skills for producing a video and getting acquainted with the cinematic tools, with the basics of editing sound and image and to get to know the video equipment and technical and professional terms used in the field.

Approach

It all started with brainstorming and with trying to find a way to express my friend's personality in a video of only 2 minutes length. The unexpectedly bad weather and a very tight schedule pushed us to improvise our script and film inside. Her passion for old cinematography gave me the idea to edit the movie using the Super 8mm style, and her flu and lack of voice pushed me to come up with the idea behind subtitles instead of a voiceover.
