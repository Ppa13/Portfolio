Brief

Fremmed is a post-punk metal band from Denmark. With the upcoming of a new EP, our team collaborated with the band on building an entire brand identity for them. The products that were delivered to this client were a music video, a landing page, a design manual, a PR package and a SoMe strategy.

Approach

Developing products for a very general target group and with time and human limitations was a bit of a challenge, but was solved through solid research and consistent communication with the client. With no budget for the video, we had to get creative at times, like for eg. using a vape to create the illusion of smoke.

Contribution

My task was to code the landing page and develop the SoMe strategy. The concept and design of the landing page were born to sustain the style of the video promoted and the band's new identity. The choice of having vertically arranged buttons came based on the band's name, which in danish it means "unfamiliar".
