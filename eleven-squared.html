Brief

The purpose of this project was to get us familiar with the Lean Startup approach in building new businesses based on the belief that entrepreneurs must investigate, experiment, test, and iterate as they develop products. Other concepts we got introduced to in this project were SEO and digital analytics.

Approach

After building one MVP and having the conclusions of the tests show us the best idea is to pivot, 11squared was born. We design 11 different pieces each month for the t-shirts we sell, with the key being, there will only ever be 121 of them printed. This way we can differentiate our business from our competitors.

Contribution

I contributed to the persuasive design of the website, the design, and the development of it. For the development part, we work on a template received from the teachers, that we had to get to understand and further develop to transform it in a webshop that we deploy and market to achieve our project's KPI's.
