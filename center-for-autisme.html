Brief

The project's scope was to redesign the website of a center for autism. The site was not user-friendly, had confusing and overwhelming IA, outdated design and it was not search engine optimized. The main focus was the IA of the website, the UX, SEO and the social media marketing strategy and content plan.

Approach

After research on the competitors and the target audience, we proceeded with the design manual and the marketing strategy. The IA of the site was redesigned to achieve a flat-site hierarchy and a better UX. We worked on obtaining consistency in their digital presence and improve the SEO ranking for their website.

Contribution

I participated in the creation of every deliverable, with the coding part of the project as my main task. I used Bootstrap for the first time and because of the extent of the navigation, comprised of several submenus, and my lack of experience, I encountered several problems that consumed a lot of my time.
