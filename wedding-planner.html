Brief

For this project we developed a wedding planner tool that will cater to the market in Denmark. We had to do a market research and develop a product based on quantitative and qualitative data. The main focus of this project was gaining a better knowledge of the Design Thinking methodology and UX.

Approach

The research carried a huge impact on the end product so a large amount of time and human resources were spent on interviewing the target audience. After prototyping we've iterated several times through the design thinking process in order to improve and tailor the product to best fit out target audience's needs.

Contribution

My contribution to this project was present in all stages of the design thinking process, with my main task being the development of the website. The challenge in this project was the time constraint and a large number of changes that appeared after each test performed on the prototype.
